Recipe: Fluffy Pancakes
Servings: 4
Time: 20 minutes

Ingredients:
- 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
- 3 1/2 tsp baking powder
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1 1/4 cups milk
- 1 egg
- 3 tbsp melted butter
- 1 tsp vanilla extract

Steps:
1. Mix flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt in a bowl.
2. In another bowl whisk milk, egg, butter, and vanilla.
3. Combine wet and dry ingredients until just mixed.
4. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat.
5. Pour 1/4 cup batter per pancake.
6. Flip when bubbles appear and edges look dry.

Serving ideas:
- Maple syrup and banana slices.
- Yogurt and berries.
