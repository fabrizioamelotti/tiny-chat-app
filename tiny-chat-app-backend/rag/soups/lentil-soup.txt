Recipe: Lentil Soup
Servings: 4
Time: 45 minutes

Ingredients:
- 1 cup dried lentils, rinsed
- 1 onion, diced
- 2 carrots, diced
- 2 celery stalks, diced
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1/2 tsp paprika
- 1.2 L vegetable broth
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Juice of 1/2 lemon

Steps:
1. Saute onion, carrot, and celery in olive oil for 6 minutes.
2. Add garlic, cumin, and paprika. Cook 1 minute.
3. Add lentils and broth. Bring to a boil.
4. Simmer 30 minutes until lentils are tender.
5. Season with salt, pepper, and lemon juice.
6. Blend partially for a creamier texture if desired.
