Recipe: Classic Tomato Pasta
Servings: 2
Time: 25 minutes

Ingredients:
- 200 g spaghetti
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 400 g canned crushed tomato
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 1/2 tsp sugar
- 6 basil leaves

Steps:
1. Boil salted water and cook spaghetti until al dente.
2. Heat olive oil in a pan and cook garlic for 30 seconds.
3. Add tomato, salt, pepper, and sugar. Simmer for 12 minutes.
4. Add torn basil leaves and stir.
5. Toss pasta with sauce and serve.

Tips:
- Save 1/4 cup pasta water to adjust sauce texture.
