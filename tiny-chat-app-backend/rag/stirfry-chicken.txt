Recipe: Chicken and Vegetable Stir-Fry
Servings: 3
Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients:
- 450 g chicken breast, sliced
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tsp cornstarch
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- 1 red bell pepper, sliced
- 1 carrot, julienned
- 1 cup broccoli florets
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 1 tsp grated ginger

Sauce:
- 3 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp oyster sauce
- 1 tsp honey
- 2 tbsp water

Steps:
1. Marinate chicken with soy sauce and cornstarch for 10 minutes.
2. Mix all sauce ingredients in a bowl.
3. Heat oil in a wok and cook chicken until golden. Remove.
4. Stir-fry vegetables for 4-5 minutes.
5. Add garlic and ginger for 30 seconds.
6. Return chicken, add sauce, and cook 2 minutes.
7. Serve with steamed rice.
