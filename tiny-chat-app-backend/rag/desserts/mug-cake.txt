Recipe: Chocolate Mug Cake
Servings: 1
Time: 5 minutes

Ingredients:
- 4 tbsp flour
- 2 tbsp cocoa powder
- 2 tbsp sugar
- 1/4 tsp baking powder
- Pinch of salt
- 3 tbsp milk
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- 1 tbsp chocolate chips

Steps:
1. Add dry ingredients to a large mug and mix.
2. Add milk and oil. Stir until smooth.
3. Add chocolate chips.
4. Microwave 60-80 seconds at high power.
5. Rest 1 minute before eating.

Notes:
- Do not overcook or it becomes dry.
